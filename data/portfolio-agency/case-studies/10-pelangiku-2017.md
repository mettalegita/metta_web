### Overview

Pelangiku is an Indonesian children's song created as part of a collaborative project celebrating imagination and creativity through music. The piece was composed by Metta Legita and Ali Akbar Sugiri with lyrics written by Thalita, sung by Adna.

### Artistic Concept

The music captures a playful and uplifting spirit, inspired by the colorful imagery of a rainbow ("pelangi" in Indonesian). Through light melodies and cheerful rhythms, the song invites young listeners to experience music as a joyful and expressive form of storytelling.

### Recording & Collaboration

This project brought together artists and collaborators to create a vibrant musical work designed for children and families, highlighting Legita's versatility as a composer across different musical contexts.
