### Overview

Home With You is a collaborative songwriting project created with Carrie Herbert and friends. The piece explores themes of connection, belonging, and emotional warmth through a contemporary pop-inspired sound.

### Recording & Collaboration

This project brought together a group of collaborators, combining songwriting, vocal performance, and musical arrangement to create a warm and accessible pop composition.
