### Artistic Concepta

The music of Contra Indigo focused on energetic grooves, expressive ensemble playing, and jazz-influenced harmonies. Through collaborative arrangements and dynamic interaction between instruments, the band created a vibrant sound that combined rhythmic drive with melodic creativity.

### Band & Musical Journey

The group featured musicians from Bandung, including Bubu Anggraeni (vocal), Taufik Eka (bass), Yuriandra Bayu (drums), Metta Legita (keyboard), and Keke Kandrianta (guitar), along with several guest musicians. During its active years, Contra Indigo performed at numerous venues and festivals across Indonesia, including 5 times appearances at the renowned Java Jazz Festival.
The band’s album received a nomination at the Anugerah Musik Indonesia, recognizing the group’s contribution to Indonesia’s contemporary music scene.

### A Musical Chapter

Although the band is no longer active today, Contra Indigo remains a meaningful part of Metta Legita’s artistic history. The project represents a formative period of collaboration, performance, and musical exploration that helped shape her path as a pianist and composer.
