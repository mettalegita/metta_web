### Overview

Cikaracak is a flute and piano composition created by Metta Legita in collaboration with Jennifer Bird. The project was released as part of a mini album during the global COVID period.

### Artistic Concept

Inspired by Indonesian musical imagery and natural rhythmic patterns, the piece explores the expressive interaction between flute and piano. The music reflects a sense of calm reflection during a time of global uncertainty.

### Recording & Collaboration

The recording features flute and piano, highlighting the intimate chamber music dialogue between the two instruments.

### Album Information

Cikaracak - Piano and Flute Music Composition by Metta Legita & Jennifer Bird. Album: Cikaracak (2021). Metta and Jennifer have performed together in many concerts and projects in Phnom Penh during the last few years. Cikaracak was recorded and produced in an intimate setting at home — a befitting testament to musical continuity in exceptional times.
