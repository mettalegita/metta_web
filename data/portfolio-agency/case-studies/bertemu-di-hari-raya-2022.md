### Overview

Bertemu di Hari Raya is a collaborative musical project created with vocalist Sharliza Jelita. The song celebrates themes of reunion, celebration, and cultural connection.

### Artistic Concept

Inspired by the festive atmosphere of Hari Raya, the piece reflects the joy of meeting loved ones and celebrating shared traditions through music.

### Recording & Collaboration

The project features collaboration with vocalist Sharliza Jelita, combining vocal performance with Legita's musical composition and arrangement.
