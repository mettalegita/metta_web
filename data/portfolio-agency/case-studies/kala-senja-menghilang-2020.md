### Overview

Kala Senja Menghilang is a collaborative musical project between Indonesian band The Panic and pianist Metta Legita. The piece blends contemporary band sound with expressive piano elements.

### Artistic Concept

The music evokes the atmosphere of twilight and transition suggested by its title ("When the dusk disappears"). Through lyrical melodies and expressive textures, the piece captures a reflective and emotional mood.

### Recording & Collaboration

The recording features collaboration with the Indonesian band The Panic, combining band instrumentation with Legita's piano performance to create a rich musical dialogue.
