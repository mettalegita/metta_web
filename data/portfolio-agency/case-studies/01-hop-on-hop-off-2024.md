### Overview

Hop On, Hop Off is a contemporary jazz composition by pianist and composer Metta Legita, written for piano and orchestra. The piece combines energetic rhythms, jazz harmonies, and interlocking melodic ideas. Recorded with the Budapest Scoring Orchestra, the performance features Legita herself as the pianist.

### Artistic Concept

The piece explores the meeting point between jazz-inspired piano writing and orchestral color. Through rhythmic drive and layered melodies, the piano interacts with the orchestra in a lively musical dialogue.

### Recording & Collaboration

The recording was produced with the Budapest Scoring Orchestra and conducted by Zoltán Pad, bringing together a vibrant ensemble sound that highlights the piece's orchestral textures.
