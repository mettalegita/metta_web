### Service Overview

I offer comprehensive piano instruction combining classical technique with contemporary jazz influences. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced player looking to refine your skills, I tailor lessons to your individual goals, learning style, and musical interests.

### What I Teach

**Piano Fundamentals:**
- Proper hand positioning and technique
- Music theory and reading notation
- Rhythm and timing development
- Ear training and musicality

**Repertoire Styles:**
- Classical piano literature
- Jazz and contemporary pieces
- Popular songs and arrangements
- Improvisation and composition basics

**Skill Development:**
- Beginner to advanced level instruction
- Performance preparation and stage confidence
- Technique building and musical expression
- Exam preparation (if desired)

### Lesson Structure

**Individual Lessons:** 45-60 minutes of focused, one-on-one instruction
**Flexible Scheduling:** Weekday evenings and weekend availability
**Online Options:** Video conferencing lessons for remote students
**Progress Tracking:** Regular assessment and goal setting

### Why Choose My Piano Lessons

**Experienced Educator:** Professional pianist with extensive teaching experience
**Personalized Approach:** Lessons adapted to your learning pace and musical interests
**Comprehensive Method:** Classical foundation with contemporary applications
**Patient and Encouraging:** Creating a supportive learning environment

Whether you're learning for personal enjoyment, preparing for performances, or pursuing a musical career, I can help you develop your piano skills and musical confidence.
